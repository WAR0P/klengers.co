Software Developer (Node.js/Vue.js)

Level: Intermediate

We are seeking a dedicated and talented Software Developer specializing in Node.js and Vue.js to join our dynamic team. As a Developer, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining web applications using Node.js and Vue.js. You will write reusable, testable, and efficient code, and design and implement low-latency, high-availability, and performant applications.

Your role will involve integrating data from various back-end services and databases, requiring a deep understanding of server-side logic and database structures. You will work closely with other developers, designers, and stakeholders to create seamless and robust applications that meet our business needs. Your ability to troubleshoot and resolve issues swiftly will ensure the smooth operation of our web services.

Qualifications:

  • Education: A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field is required. This educational background ensures you have a strong foundation in the principles of software development.
  • Experience: Proven experience as a Node.js and Vue.js developer in a commercial environment is essential. This means you should have hands-on experience in developing and maintaining web applications using these technologies.
  • Technical Skills: Familiarity with various development tools and frameworks related to Node.js and Vue.js is crucial. You should be experienced in using tools like npm, Webpack, and various Node.js and Vue.js libraries and frameworks.
  • Best Practices: A strong understanding of best practices in software development is required. This includes knowledge of design patterns, code quality standards, version control with Git, and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) practices.
  • Analytical Skills: Strong analytical and problem-solving skills are essential. You should be able to analyze complex application requirements, identify potential issues, and develop effective solutions. Attention to detail is critical to ensure the quality and performance of our applications.
  • Communication Skills: Excellent verbal and written communication skills are necessary for documenting code, writing clear commit messages, and articulating technical concepts. You should be able to work collaboratively with other developers, designers, and stakeholders.
  • Adaptability: The ability to quickly learn new technologies and adapt to changing requirements is important. The IT industry is dynamic, and you should be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment with evolving project needs.
